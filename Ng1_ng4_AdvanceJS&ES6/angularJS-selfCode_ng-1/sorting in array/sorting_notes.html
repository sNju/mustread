There are basically 5 ways to sort numbers in JavaScript- sort()- this will sort numbers normally. asort()- this will sort an array of numbers in ascending order based on their values. arsort()- this will sort an array of numbers in descending order based on their values. ksort()- this will sort an array of numbers in ascending order based on their key values. krsort()- this will sort an array of numbers in descending order based on their key values.
